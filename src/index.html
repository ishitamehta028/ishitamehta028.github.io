Often, I find it difficult to ask others for help, and sit with my struggles alone, not wanting to burden others with my problems, but I’m a work in progress and try to become a better version of myself every single day.

Life is about connections and conversations. I genuinely hope to make meaningful connections, acquire skills, seek new opportunities & broaden my perspective with ideas unheard of. I also wish to have different enriching experiences that fuel my appetite for knowledge to help me grow into a self-sufficient individual.
